## Welcome to EPIC Instructor App

The EPIC Instructor App is specifically designed for padel coaches, instructors, and trainers to manage their lessons, track student progress, and streamline their coaching business.

### Key Features

- **Lesson Scheduling**: Manage your coaching calendar and availability
- **Student Management**: Track student progress and development
- **Session Planning**: Create and organize training programs
- **Payment Tracking**: Monitor lesson payments and earnings
- **Performance Analytics**: Analyze student improvement and coaching effectiveness
- **Communication Tools**: Stay connected with students and club management

### Instructor Tools

- **Lesson Templates**: Pre-built training sessions and drills
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor student skill development over time
- **Booking Management**: Handle lesson bookings and cancellations
- **Resource Library**: Access coaching materials and training resources
- **Certification Tracking**: Manage instructor credentials and certifications
