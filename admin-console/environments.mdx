## Admin Console Environments

Access the EPIC Admin Console through the following environments based on your needs:

### Production Environment
**URL**: [https://dashboard.epic-padel.com/](https://dashboard.epic-padel.com/)

The live production environment for managing your padel club operations. Use this for:
- Daily club management tasks
- Live member and booking management
- Real-time reporting and analytics
- Production staff operations

### QA Environment
**URL**: [https://epic-admin-dashboard.mvp-apps.ae/](https://epic-admin-dashboard.mvp-apps.ae/)

Quality assurance environment for testing new features and configurations before deploying to production. Use this for:
- Testing new features and updates
- Training new staff members
- Validating configurations before production deployment
- Safe experimentation with settings

## QA Credentials
