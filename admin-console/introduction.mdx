## Welcome to EPIC Admin Console

The EPIC Admin Console is the comprehensive administrative dashboard designed for club managers and administrators to oversee all aspects of padel club operations.

### Key Features

- **Club Management**: Complete oversight of club facilities and operations
- **Member Management**: Handle member registrations, profiles, and memberships
- **Court Scheduling**: Manage court availability and booking systems
- **Staff Management**: Oversee instructors, staff schedules, and roles
- **Financial Dashboard**: Track revenue, payments, and financial analytics
- **Reporting & Analytics**: Generate detailed reports on club performance

### Administrative Tools

- **User Access Control**: Manage permissions and user roles
- **System Configuration**: Configure club settings and preferences
- **Maintenance Scheduling**: Track facility maintenance and equipment
- **Event Management**: Organize tournaments and special events
- **Communication Center**: Send notifications and announcements
- **Integration Management**: Connect with external systems and APIs
