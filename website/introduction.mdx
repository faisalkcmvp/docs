## Welcome to EPIC Website

The EPIC Website serves as the central web platform for padel club management, providing comprehensive tools for club operations, member management, and business analytics.

### Key Features

- **Club Management**: Comprehensive dashboard for club operations
- **Member Portal**: Web-based access for members and players
- **Booking System**: Online court reservation system
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Business insights and performance metrics
- **Content Management**: Club information, events, and announcements
- **Integration Hub**: Connect with mobile apps and third-party services

### Platform Components

- **Public Website**: Marketing and information pages for potential members
- **Member Portal**: Authenticated area for existing members
- **Admin Dashboard**: Management tools for club administrators
- **API Endpoints**: Integration capabilities for external systems
