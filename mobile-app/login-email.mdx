## Email Login

Use your registered email address and password to access your EPIC Padel account.

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Open the EPIC Mobile App**
   - Launch the app on your device
   - Tap "Login" on the welcome screen

2. **Select Email Login**
   - Choose "Login with Email" option
   - You'll see the email login form

3. **Enter Your Credentials**
   - **Email**: Enter your registered email address
   - **Password**: Type your account password
   - Ensure both fields are filled correctly

4. **Sign In**
   - Tap the "Login" button
   - Wait for authentication to complete

### Forgot Your Password?

If you can't remember your password:

1. Tap "Forgot Password?" on the login screen
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Follow the link to create a new password
5. Return to the app and login with your new password

### Troubleshooting

**Login Failed?**
- Double-check your email address for typos
- Ensure your password is correct (case-sensitive)
- Check your internet connection
- Try logging in again after a few minutes

**Account Not Found?**
- Verify you're using the correct email address
- You may need to create a new account if you haven't registered yet
- Contact support if you believe your account should exist

<Note>
Make sure you're using the same email address you used when creating your EPIC Padel account.
</Note>

### Security Tips

- Use a strong, unique password for your account
- Don't share your login credentials with others
- Log out when using shared devices
- Enable two-factor authentication if available
