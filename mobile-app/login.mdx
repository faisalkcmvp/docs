## Login to EPIC Mobile App

The EPIC Mobile App offers multiple convenient ways to access your account. Choose the login method that works best for you.

### Available Login Methods

- **Email Login**: Use your registered email address and password
- **Phone Login**: Sign in using your phone number with SMS verification
- **Social Accounts**: Quick login with Google or Apple accounts

### Getting Started

1. Download the EPIC Mobile App from your device's app store
2. Open the app and tap "Login" on the welcome screen
3. Choose your preferred login method
4. Follow the authentication steps for your selected method
