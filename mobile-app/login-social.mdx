## Social Account Login

Sign in quickly and securely using your existing Google or Apple account credentials.

### Available Social Login Options

- **Google Account**: Use your Gmail or Google account
- **Apple ID**: Sign in with your Apple ID (iOS devices)

### Google Account Login

1. **Select Google Login**
   - Tap "Continue with Google" on the login screen
   - You'll be redirected to Google's secure login page

2. **Authenticate with Google**
   - Enter your Google email and password
   - Complete any two-factor authentication if enabled
   - Grant permission for EPIC to access basic profile info

3. **Account Linking**
   - If this is your first time, your Google account will be linked to EPIC
   - Future logins will be automatic with Google
   - You'll be redirected back to the EPIC app

### Apple ID Login

1. **Select Apple Login**
   - Tap "Continue with Apple" on the login screen
   - Available on iOS devices with iOS 13+ and macOS 10.15+

2. **Authenticate with Apple**
   - Use Face ID, Touch ID, or your device passcode
   - Choose to share or hide your email address
   - Apple will create a private relay email if you choose to hide

3. **Complete Setup**
   - Your Apple ID will be securely linked to your EPIC account
   - Future logins will use biometric authentication
   - Return to the EPIC app to start using your account

### First-Time Social Login

When logging in with a social account for the first time:

1. **Account Creation**
   - A new EPIC account will be created automatically
   - Your profile information will be populated from your social account
   - You may need to complete additional profile setup

2. **Privacy Settings**
   - Review what information is shared from your social account
   - Only basic profile info (name, email) is typically used
   - You can modify privacy settings in your EPIC profile

### Linking Existing Accounts

If you already have an EPIC account:

1. **Account Merge**
   - Log in with your existing email/phone method first
   - Go to Account Settings > Linked Accounts
   - Add your Google or Apple account for future quick login

2. **Multiple Login Methods**
   - You can use email, phone, or social login interchangeably
   - All methods access the same EPIC account and data
   - Choose the most convenient method for each login

### Troubleshooting Social Login

**Google Login Issues:**
- Ensure you're using the correct Google account
- Check that you have a stable internet connection
- Clear your browser cache if using web version
- Try logging out of Google and back in

**Apple ID Issues:**
- Verify your Apple ID is active and verified
- Ensure your device supports Apple Sign-In
- Check that you're signed into iCloud on your device
- Update to the latest iOS version if needed

**Permission Errors:**
- Review and accept the required permissions
- EPIC only requests basic profile information
- You can revoke permissions in your Google/Apple account settings

<Note>
Social login uses industry-standard OAuth 2.0 security protocols to protect your account information.
</Note>

### Security & Privacy

- **Secure Authentication**: Social login uses secure OAuth protocols
- **Limited Data Access**: Only basic profile info is accessed
- **No Password Storage**: EPIC doesn't store your social account passwords
- **Easy Revocation**: You can disconnect social accounts anytime in settings

### Benefits of Social Login

- **Faster Access**: No need to remember another password
- **Enhanced Security**: Leverage Google/Apple's security features
- **Simplified Management**: One less account to manage
- **Biometric Support**: Use Face ID/Touch ID on supported devices
