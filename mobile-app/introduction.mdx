## Welcome to EPIC Mobile App

The EPIC Mobile App is designed for padel players and club members to easily book courts, manage their sessions, and connect with the padel community.

### Key Features

- **Court Booking**: Reserve padel courts at your preferred times
- **Session Management**: Track your games and playing history  
- **Player Profiles**: Connect with other padel enthusiasts
- **Club Information**: Access club details, amenities, and schedules
- **Payment Integration**: Secure payment processing for bookings
- **Notifications**: Stay updated on bookings, cancellations, and club news

### Getting Started

Download the EPIC Mobile App from your device's app store and create your player account to start booking courts and joining the padel community.
